Web analysis with Piwik

This website uses Piwik Analytics, a web analysis service of the Radon Cloud. Piwik Analytics uses cookies, i.e. text files stored on your computer to enable analysis of website usage by you. Information generated by the cookie about your use of this website is usually transmitted to a server in Amsterdam, Netherlands and stored there. Because of activated IP anonymization on this website, however, your IP address is previously truncated within member states of the European Union or in other states which are party to the agreement on the European Economic Area. Only in exceptional cases is a full IP address transmitted to the server and truncated there. On behalf this website's owner, the Radon Cloud will use this information to evaluate your use of the website, compile reports about website activities, and provide the website's operator with further services related to website and Internet usage. The IP address sent from your browser as part of Analytics is not merged with other data by the Radon Cloud. You can prevent storage of cookies by appropriately setting your browser software; in this case, however, please note that you might not be able to fully use all functions offered by this website.

This website uses Analytics with the extension "anonymizeIP()", IP addresses being truncated before further processing in order to rule out direct associations to persons. For Example your Public IP, 93.217.49.122, is transmitted and saved as 93.217.0.0.


Information about cookies

(1) To optimize my web presence, I use cookies. These are small text files stored in your computer's main memory. These cookies are deleted after you close the browser. (2) You can prevent storage of cookies by choosing a "disable cookies" option in your browser settings. But this can limit the functionality of my Internet offers as a result.
